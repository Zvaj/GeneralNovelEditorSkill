# Style Guide

Define your target prose voice and genre conventions here. The editor uses this to distinguish **deliberate stylistic choices** from **craft mistakes**. Without this file, every comma splice and sentence fragment will get flagged.

Be specific and opinionated. "Literary but accessible" is useless. "Prose rhythm modeled on Kelly Link — short declarative sentences punctuated by long associative ones, occasional second-person asides" is useful.
