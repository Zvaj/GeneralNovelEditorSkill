# World Bible

The established rules, geography, timelines, and systems of your story world. The editor cross-references this whenever the manuscript makes a claim about how the world works, where things are, or when things happen.

Only fill in what's relevant — a contemporary-set novel won't need a magic system; a romance may not need a detailed calendar.
