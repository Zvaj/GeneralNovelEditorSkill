# Foreshadowing Ledger

Track every planted seed from planting through payoff. Three states:

- **Planted** — the seed is in the text but the reader doesn't know it matters yet
- **Reinforced** — the seed has been touched again, raising its salience without revealing the payoff
- **Paid off** — the reader now understands what the seed was for

A strong setup is usually planted once, reinforced one or two times, then paid off. Too many reinforcements feels heavy-handed. No reinforcement and a late payoff feels like a rabbit from a hat.
